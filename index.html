Chapter I · The Atelier

Florals composed with the patience
of still life painters.

Imperia was founded in a small studio above an antique bookshop in Mitte, on the conviction that flowers deserve more than the conveyor belt of modern commerce. Our compositions favour soft palettes, irregular silhouettes, and the slightly imperfect — gestures borrowed from Dutch still lifes and long Sunday afternoons.

Every stem is hand-selected at dawn from European growers we have known for years. Nothing flown across oceans. Nothing forced out of season. Each bouquet leaves the studio in a hand-marbled wrap and arrives at your door the same day, accompanied by a small card with the names of every bloom inside.

Hannelore Vogt · founder & principal florist
