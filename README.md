# IMPERIA — Maison Florale

*A boutique floral atelier · Berlin Mitte · est. MMXIV*

> **Demonstration project.** IMPERIA is a fictional business invented for portfolio purposes. The studio, the address, the testimonials, the press clippings, the bouquets, and the prices are all made up. No flowers were sold in the making of this website.

---

## What this is

A production-grade marketing site for a *hypothetical* European floral atelier — designed and built end-to-end as a study in editorial, art-directed e-commerce. Every detail is hand-tuned: the typography, the choreography of the page load, the way a bouquet card lifts on hover, the rhythm of the spacing between sections, the soft cream paper grain that sits over everything.

The intent: prove that a static site, with no framework and no build step, can still feel like the cover of a magazine.

## A studio that doesn't exist, photographed as if it did

Imperia is the imagined work of *Hannelore Vogt* and *Mira Lindgren-Holt* — two florists who, in this little universe, run a small atelier above an antique bookshop in Mitte. They source from European growers at dawn. They hand-tie everything in marbled wrap. They write notes by hand. They have a quarterly workshop with eight guests and natural wine.

None of this is real. All of it is rendered in such loving detail that, by the time you reach the studio's hours and the pulsing pin on the hand-drawn map of Mitte, you may forget that for a moment.
